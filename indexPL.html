AThere’s no way around seeing incremental fluctuations in your rankings both up and down. That is a normal part of how Google’s ranking algorithm works. Over time, your rankings will even out and you should see rankings hold stronger as you continue to build links and drive authority to your site.

We start with auditing the site to fix any issues that might be holding it back. We always want to make sure we can get a clean crawl and Google isn’t devaluing the site in any way. Once the audit is finished, we optimize the site for your target keywords and phrases. From there, we start building authority to the site through our manual link building efforts.

An example of a backlink is when one website links to another, essentially giving that website a vote of confidence. Backlinks are the lifeblood of SEO and the largest part of Googles ranking algorithm. The more quality links and signals you’re able to show Google, the higher you tend to rank provided everything was done correctly. Link building should be done delicately and only by experts in the SEO field.
